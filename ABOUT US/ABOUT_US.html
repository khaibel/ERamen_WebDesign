About Us

絵ラーメン屋の原点

Established in 1910 by the Koyama Family, ERamen (絵ラーメン屋) is known as one of the oldest ramen to date. With original hand-made noodles made from organic flour, ERamen has now reached over 100 branch in 3 countries.

1910

ERamen (絵ラーメン屋) first established in the suburb of yokohama by Koyama Keiichiro as the Koyama-family business.

1960

Opened our first Tokyo branch as a 50 year-anniversary celebration

1970

Opened our first Hokkaido branch in Hakodate

1983

Opened our first kansai-region branch in Osaka

1985

Opened our first traditionally-designed branch in Kyoto

1999

Won "TV champion - Ramen Master Chef Championships" aired by TV Tokyo

2007

Started global expansion by opening our first overseas-branch in Taiwan.

2008

Opened our okinawa branch with a partnership with Hiyori Ocean Resort Okinawa

2009

Opened out first Ginza branch as a 100 store commemoration

2010

Gave out 100 free ramen from each branch as part of our 100 year anniversary celebration

2015

Opened our first Indonesia store in Jakarta

2020

Opened our first nation-wide online store to accommodate the pandemic
